Our Story

Cape Town's Artificial Grass Specialists

FakeGrass Cape Town is one of South Africa's leading professional synthetic turf companies, based right here in Cape Town. We were founded on a simple belief: Cape Town homeowners and businesses deserve premium quality artificial grass, installed correctly, without shortcuts.

Led by Stefan and a team of experienced installers, we've spent over 15 years mastering the art of artificial grass installation across the Western Cape. From intimate balcony gardens in Sea Point to large commercial grounds in the Northern Suburbs — we bring the same level of care and craftsmanship to every project.

We take pride in offering only premium quality products and exceptional customer service. Our no shortcut policy when it comes to ground preparation means your installation is done right the first time — and it shows in how long our lawns look beautiful.

We believe Cape Town's water-conscious environment is the perfect match for high-quality synthetic turf. Your garden stays lush through our hot, dry summers and our windy, wet winters — without a drop of water or a minute of maintenance.
