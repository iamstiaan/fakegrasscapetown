FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does artificial grass cost in Cape Town?

Pricing depends on the area size, product grade, and site preparation required. Residential installations typically start from R150–R250/m² installed. Contact us for a detailed, transparent quote specific to your project.

How long does installation take?

Most residential installations take 1–2 days. Larger commercial or sports projects may take 3–5 days. We'll give you a clear timeline in your written quote.

Is your artificial grass safe for children and pets?

Absolutely. All our products are lead-free, non-toxic, and specifically engineered to be safe for children and pets. We do not use any harmful chemicals in our products.

What warranty do you offer?

Our premium artificial grass range carries an 8-year manufacturer's warranty. We also provide a workmanship guarantee on all our installations.

Will artificial grass look natural?

Yes! Our advanced fibre technology and natural colour blends are designed to closely replicate real grass. Many people genuinely cannot tell the difference up close.

Can fake grass handle Cape Town's weather?

Yes. Our products are UV-stabilised for the South African sun, frost-resistant for Cape winters, and wind-resistant for the infamous South-Easter. Engineered specifically for our climate.
